The header has the class of "site-header' and 'shrig-hand', site-header ensures that the header does not exceed the size of the flexbox, being 0 and shrig-hand establishes the font and its details. The div class 'themeing' addresses the color selector in the bottom left corner. it has a lot of styling such as flexbox, positioning, and z -index.
